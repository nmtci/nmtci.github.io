# Chapter 851: The Feather Book's Whereabouts

After some thought, Bu Yingshu already had a plan in mind.

"Celestial Inquiry Star, what should I do next?"

Celestial Inquiry Star: "I have understood the user's situation. I suggest the user have an open and honest talk with Zhang Yu and convey your requirements to him..."

Hearing the Celestial Inquiry Star's answer, Bu Yingshu angrily said, "If it were that simple, why would I ask you? I want you to tell me a method that will definitely succeed and allow me to fiercely control Zhang Yu! Don't give me these messy template answers."

Saying that, Bu Yingshu directly tipped the Celestial Inquiry Star 1,000 Immortal Coins and continued, "Give me a proper answer."

The Celestial Inquiry Star immediately replied: "You said it so well, it's as if you suddenly struck my heart. I understand, I completely understand. Next, I won't use templates, won't evade, won't hide, won't beat around the bush, and will only communicate with you in the most authentic way..."

Celestial Inquiry Star: "If the user wants to control Zhang Yu, the three safest methods are: first, money; second, power; and third, the ubiquitous rules in Kunxu..."

Looking at the Celestial Inquiry Star's answer, a trace of thoughtfulness appeared in Bu Yingshu's eyes. "Throwing money is indeed the most convenient method."

Scenes of memories surfaced in her mind. It was exactly the time when she and Zhang Yu were researching cultivation arts and refining techniques together in the laboratory.

Bu Yingshu still remembered that back then, she only needed to throw a bit of money at Zhang Yu every day, and he would obediently listen to her.

"Sigh, Zhang Yu was so obedient back then. Now this kid has more and more power. He might control the industries of an entire minor realm in the future, but he's also becoming increasingly disobedient."

Thinking of this, Bu Yingshu couldn't help but stomp her feet, as if she were violently trampling Zhang Yu on the ground.

"Now, if I want him to put down his work in the Development Zone and obediently accompany me, it probably won't be achievable with just a little money."

Thus, Bu Yingshu thought of power again.

"Although my rank is higher than his, I belong to the Martial Arts Pavilion, so I have absolutely no authority over the current Zhang Yu."

In particular, Bu Yingshu thought of the new regulations of the Development Zone Management Committee, which prohibited outsiders like her from entering, whereas that sword brute Bai Zhenzhen could openly go in and out.

Bu Yingshu was both angry and anxious. "What should I do? Rules... What rules of Kunxu can I use?"

In the next moment, Bu Yingshu's gaze towards the Celestial Inquiry Star suddenly brightened. "Right, rules! Hidden rules!"

"I am the representative of the Myriad Laws Immortal Clan investing in the Development Zone!"

"Wouldn't it be perfectly justified for me to subject Zhang Yu to the hidden rules?"

As a member of the Myriad Laws Immortal Clan, the ruling class, and at the Void Refinement Realm, Bu Yingshu had some understanding of the hidden rules within Kunxu.

It's just that in the past, she didn't need to care about any hidden rules most of the time.

But she also knew that in this Kunxu, as an investment representative, it was completely natural for her to apply the hidden rules to the "investee".

And the targets of these hidden rules, whether enterprises or departments, were naturally ranked according to cultivation and assets, and often the big leaders in the company or the top heads of the department would be the first to be subjected to the hidden rules.

Right now, the Myriad Laws Immortal Clan was investing in the Development Zone through Zhang Yu's channel, so Zhang Yu was naturally the primary target of her, Bu Yingshu's hidden rules.

"Hmph, this kid's next primary task... is to develop the Soul Cultivator Industry in the Development Zone and increase the share of our Myriad Laws Sect, right?"

"If he wants to achieve results and continue to be promoted in the future, this investment from our Myriad Laws Immortal Clan is crucial."

"In this way... as the investment representative, can't I take control of his Promotion Accelerator?"

Thinking of this, a gleam flashed in Bu Yingshu's eyes. It was as if she could already see herself completely taking control of Zhang Yu once again, seeing him obediently stimulate his potential for her, seeing herself become the existence with the number one talent in the Myriad Laws Immortal Clan, and seeing the Myriad Laws Immortal Emperor descend, pointing at her with great praise: "This child is like me!"

"Little Yu, don't think you can escape the palm of my hand just because you've grasped a bit more power and obtained a Superior Merit and a Death-Exempting Golden Token."

"No matter how great your power is, your roots are ultimately still on the land of Myriad Laws."

"From now on, if you don't accompany me to stimulate my potential for twelve hours a day, don't even think about having me advance the investment process for you."

Thinking of this, Bu Yingshu had already clicked on Zhang Yu's avatar, requesting an immediate call.

"Little Yu, what are you doing?"

"Dao Monarch, I am working."

Inside the Immortal Grotto-Heaven, Zhang Yu was currently situated within the Grotto-Heaven while also existing in the Former Days Graveyard.

With the tight connection between the Immortal Grotto-Heaven and the Former Days Graveyard, Zhang Yu, after borrowing the power of the Immortal Grotto-Heaven, could keep himself in a superimposed state between the two spaces.

This allowed him to touch the matter inside the Immortal Grotto-Heaven at any time, constantly receive Spirit Realm signals from the Former Days Graveyard, and instantly hide himself when he wanted to hide, or instantly reveal himself when he wanted to appear.

At this very moment, he was talking to Bu Yingshu while hurriedly patting Bai Zhenzhen's Dantian position, signaling her to hide to the side first after taking the Spirit Root.

Having reconnected her True Spirit Root, Bai Zhenzhen's body trembled slightly before she hurriedly hid to the side.

Shortly after deceiving Bu Yingshu, Bai Zhenzhen was brought into the Immortal Grotto-Heaven by Zhang Yu, where she exchanged Spirit Roots with him and returned Fu Ji.

Looking at Zhang Yu, who was respectfully conversing with Bu Yingshu, Bai Zhenzhen sighed in her heart, "Does that Bu Yingshu harass Zhang Yu this often?"

Fu Ji's voice echoed in Bai Zhenzhen's mind, "You know how it is. Zhang Yu can elevate the talents of others. The last time he researched the Sect-Guarding Technique with Bu Yingshu, he conveniently elevated her talent..."

As an Evil God who had been following Zhang Yu all this time, although she could not figure out the exact form of the potential stimulated in Zhang Yu, she had gradually deduced some patterns over the years.

For example, being able to elevate the talents of others was one of the patterns she had deduced.

Fu Ji continued, "This woman, Bu Yingshu, also got a taste of Zhang Yu's benefits. Ever since then, she has been wanting to control Zhang Yu, wishing he could accompany her every day."

Hearing this, Bai Zhenzhen nodded slightly. Having climbed from the First Layer to the sects together with Zhang Yu, she had naturally also noticed some of his abilities.

However, since Zhang Yu did not take the initiative to speak of them, she would not pry into the secrets on him.

And now... in Bai Zhenzhen's eyes, Bu Yingshu didn't just want to explore Zhang Yu's secrets, she wanted to completely control him...

"She wants to force Yu Zi to his knees, completely subjugate him, and take him back to be her slave!"

At the thought of this, Bai Zhenzhen wished she could launch a massive investigation and uncover all the taxes Bu Yingshu had evaded.

"An Immortal Clan member like Bu Yingshu... must have evaded a lot of taxes."

Bai Zhenzhen knew this was the most powerful move to deal with these Immortal Clans.

But Bai Zhenzhen also understood that with her current cultivation, authority, and assets... she absolutely couldn't do this, and might even bring trouble upon herself.

"Right now, the only way to counter Bu Yingshu is probably to divert the trouble, using that old man of the Bliss Sword Dao to fight her."

After some exchange with Zhang Yu, Bai Zhenzhen knew their attempt to fool Bu Yingshu had succeeded.

Fu Ji chuckled and said, "This Bu Yingshu has definitely been deceived by the Heavenly Sword Sect's Blissful Sword cultivators before... probably more than once."

Bai Zhenzhen nodded slightly and thought to herself, "For now... perhaps I can use the conflict between both of them to achieve a little bit of mutual checks and balances."

Seeing that Zhang Yu was still on the phone, Bai Zhenzhen waved to him and said in her heart through the Evil God Power, "Yu Zi, I will be leaving first."

Zhang Yu nodded and said to her in his heart, "Wait a moment, let me first test if Bu Yingshu is guarding outside the Development Zone."

A moment later, Bai Zhenzhen finally returned to her office.

As soon as she returned to the office, she saw the mysterious person sitting in her chair with a grinning face, "Good deception, you deceived her well. A mere Spirit Transformation actually scared away a Void Refinement of the Myriad Laws Immortal Clan. However..."

"Were you really deceiving her? Or... do you really know some secret of Bu Yingshu?"

Bai Zhenzhen said calmly, "If I really knew some big secret, I wouldn't just use it to scare her away."

"If you want to see more fun, you should pass the Bliss Sword Dao to me now."

The mysterious person grinned and smiled, "Do you know how many Immortal Coins the Bliss Sword Dao can sell for?"

Bai Zhenzhen said, "It's not a physical object anyway. How much this cultivation art costs is just a matter of you writing an approval note, isn't it?"

"But if I cultivated the Bliss Sword Dao, wouldn't it be more fun to play with Bu Yingshu?"

"Otherwise, if every contact with her is so dangerous and there's no benefit at all, I'll have to avoid her in the future."

Bai Zhenzhen was originally just saying it casually, but she didn't expect the mysterious person in front of her to suddenly quiet down, as if falling into some kind of thought and weighing.

Bai Zhenzhen was startled in her heart: "He's actually considering it?"

Bai Zhenzhen hurriedly communicated with Fu Ji and Zhang Yu through the Evil God Power, asking, "This Bu Yingshu is really special... isn't she?"

Fu Ji said, "You bet. Touching her once incurs a fine, and touching places you shouldn't could even fine someone to bankruptcy, something they couldn't pay off in nine lifetimes of Immortal Cultivation."

Fu Ji guessed, "I have a feeling that Bu Yingshu's status in the Myriad Laws Immortal Clan... might be even higher than some Tribulation Transcendence or even Ascension cultivators."

Bai Zhenzhen secretly nodded in her heart: "There is definitely something wrong with this Bu Yingshu, so much so that the high-level Supreme Bliss Cultivators of the Heavenly Sword Sect... all really want to toy with her."

Right at this moment, the mysterious person in front of her smiled and said: "Bai Zhenzhen, you have already mastered the Extreme Emotion Sword Dao and the Sorrowful Sword Dao, and now you still want to cultivate the Bliss Sword Dao... aren't you afraid of Qi Deviation?"

Bai Zhenzhen said: "Compared to Qi Deviation, I am more afraid of being poor and weak."

Bai Zhenzhen said seriously: "As long as there is an opportunity to become stronger, as long as I can earn more money, and as long as I can climb up faster, even if I have to cultivate all the Sword Daos and Sword Intents within the Heavenly Sword Sect that are driven by various emotions... I am willing to do it."

The mysterious person laughed: "Bai Zhenzhen, you are more interesting than I expected. It seems that being favored by destiny is just one of your good qualities."

"Alright then... if you want to court death, I will grant your wish. Today, I will first impart the True Inheritance version of the Blissful Sword to you. If you want a higher level of the Blissful Sword, then you will have to show me something more interesting."
