Fa Liuyuan knew that several major factions within the Myriad Laws Sect had also prepared for a long time for this third-generation Law Edict, with participating cultivators who were absolutely no weaker than Tai Yuebai.

"This fierce battle... Zhang Yu dodged it, which might not necessarily be a bad thing."

*No, Bai Zhenzhen!*

*Even though doing this would make you very happy, and even though... Zhang Yu might also really like it.*

At the same time, the Extreme Emotion Sword Qi also seemed to be driven by it, becoming restless as well.

A trace of understanding flashed through Bai Zhenzhen's eyes. *I'm afraid that if I had just followed the feeling of the Extreme Bliss Sword Intent... and transmitted the Spirit Root, the Extreme Bliss Sword Intent and Extreme Emotion Sword Intent would influence and superimpose on each other, and they would surge together...*

Meanwhile, the third burst of Sword Intent inside Bai Zhenzhen's body was also abruptly urged by her, which was exactly the Sorrowful Sword Dao she had mastered in the Heavenly Sword Sect.

The pain brought by the backlash of extreme emotion and Supreme Bliss now amplified the Grief Sword Intent, gradually reaching a new balance within Bai Zhenzhen's body.

"Right, just like this... I need to adapt to the sword qi bit by bit and harmonize the Sword Intent."

Bai Zhenzhen could feel that as long as she subdued this desire in her heart today, she would be one step closer to subduing the three great Sword Daos in her body.
