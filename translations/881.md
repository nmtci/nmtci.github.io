# Chapter 881 Deeply in Line with My Intentions, Empress Wa Creations

"You?"

Fa Liuyuan looked at the serious-faced Zhang Yu in front of him. He did not immediately deny the other party, but asked, "Are you confident that you can complete the ten Divine Transformations and break through to Void Refinement in the upcoming time?"

Zhang Yu thought for a moment and said, "I should be able to."

Fa Liuyuan had stood tall in Kunxu for many years. After becoming an Ascension Realm cultivator, he had seen generation after generation of Heavenly Chosen in the sect.

Facing Zhang Yu's confidence, he did not question him, but asked very practically, "Even if you complete the ten Divine Light Transformations and break through to the Void Refinement Realm in the following time, do you have the confidence to compete against a group of cultivators who have accumulated and prepared for many years at the peak of Void Refinement?"

Zhang Yu said, "Heavenly Monarch, I know there is a gap between them and me. Even if I break through to the Void Refinement Realm, there might still be a huge gap, but I want to give it a try."

Fa Liuyuan advised, "Next year marks the establishment of the first batch of third-generation Law Edicts. This is indeed a good opportunity, but it also represents more intense competition. If you go and participate with your cultivation, you will very likely suffer a loss."

After some thought, Fa Liuyuan simply revealed more inside information and continued, "Do not participate in this session. The faction has already decided to fully support Tai Yuebai. After he succeeds, we will fully support you in the next session."

In Fa Liuyuan's eyes, Tai Yuebai and Zhang Yu... were cultivators of two completely different generations.

Now, because the first batch of third-generation Law Edicts had begun operating ahead of schedule, Zhang Yu, as part of the new generation, was not yet fully prepared. Therefore, letting the older generation's Tai Yuebai step up first, with the two of them participating in the establishment of Law Edicts in successive sessions, was a normal arrangement for a faction.

Fa Liuyuan patted Zhang Yu on the shoulder and said, "The importance of Law Establishment concerns your future limits and path. Do not act on a momentary impulse."

Zhang Yu replied, "Heavenly Monarch, I am not acting on impulse."

He explained, "The third-generation Law Edict is a completely new domain. As you just said, the earlier one participates in Law Establishment... the larger the potential jurisdiction involved. If I miss the opportunity to establish the first batch of third-generation Law Edicts, it will be gone forever.

"So, Heavenly Monarch, with such a golden opportunity, I do not want to give up without even giving it a try."

Zhang Yu's mind drifted to the blank Law Scroll bestowed upon him by the Immortal Emperor. He knew that the earlier he participated in Law Establishment, the more he could exert the effect of this blank Law Scroll.

And participating in the inaugural establishment of the third-generation Law Edict would push this unprecedented blank Law Scroll to its absolute limit.

Zhang Yu thought to himself, "If Fa Liuyuan and the others absolutely disagree... perhaps I can only consider whether to reveal the fact that the Immortal Emperor secretly bestowed the blank Law Scroll upon me."

As these thoughts flashed through his mind, Zhang Yu looked at Fa Liuyuan and continued, "Heavenly Monarch, it is precisely because Law Establishment dictates my future upper limit on the path of the Immortal Dao. Since I want to climb to the Apex of the Immortal Dao, how could I miss out on this unique opportunity?"

Sensing the intense fighting spirit in Zhang Yu's eyes, a trace of realization suddenly flashed through Fa Liuyuan's mind, "That is right. If Zhang Yu did not possess a resolute heart toward the Dao, he presumably would not have been able to climb to this position."

"And under his aspiration for the Immortal Dao, if Zhang Yu felt that he stood absolutely no chance, then he should have just given up directly to minimize his losses, accumulate his strength, and prepare for future Law Establishment opportunities."

"But on the other hand, if he clearly knows the road ahead is treacherous, yet still has absolute confidence in himself? What would happen then? I'm afraid under the compounding influence of his aspiration for the Immortal Dao... he absolutely won't give up, right?"

"So does Zhang Yu have this kind of confidence?"

At the same time, Zhang Yu's past experiences and data floated before Fa Liuyuan's eyes. There was the Foundation Establishment Examination, his first Military Certificate, his transfer from Civil Engineering to artifact refinement, and all the data from the Ten Greats League, the University War, and the Lower Realm Sect Examination.

"Zhang Yu, who was able to climb all the way up from the First Layer, has originally completed one impossible challenge after another. Under the doubtful gazes of countless people, he defeated his opponents time and time again. He has always been winning, always proving himself. Only then did he seize countless opportunities and climb to stand before us."

"So, almost everywhere he goes, having won his way up from the very bottom... he possesses a level of confidence and pressure resistance that even the vast majority of the sects' Heavenly Chosen lack."

Thinking up to here, a flash of enlightenment crossed Fa Liuyuan's heart. "So... we still underestimated Zhang Yu a bit, treating him like those traditional Heavenly Chosen of the past."

"He is not an ordinary cultivator who simply strives forward under the influence of his aspiration for the Immortal Dao."

"He possesses extremely strong confidence and courage, firmly determined to break through to the Apex of the Immortal Dao. He also believes he can reach the Apex of the Immortal Dao, and can even, under his aspiration for the Immortal Dao... resist the short-term temptation of the Limitless Salary Increase Authority."

"When such an Immortal Talent encounters danger and hardship, he naturally doesn't think about dodging to reduce his losses, but rather about pulling chestnuts from the fire to strive for the maximum profit."

"This is indeed not a momentary impulse. Rather, the consecutive victories he has experienced in the past have shaped his indomitable courage and confidence, nourished his firm dao heart, and led to his current choice."

Fa Liuyuan thought to himself, "Under these circumstances, forcibly suppressing him and telling him to settle down a bit more might actually cause a bad result."

As a series of thoughts flashed like lightning through his mind, Fa Liuyuan gave a slight nod and said, "I understand. We previously... did indeed somewhat neglect your personal pursuit of the Apex of the Immortal Dao."

Hearing these words, Zhang Yu breathed a slight sigh of relief in his heart.

Fu Ji chuckled upon hearing this and said, "I told you there'd be no problem doing it this way, right? As long as we show our determination, he will understand! Under the Aspiration for the Immortal Dao, with the boldness and confidence you've shown, Zhang Yu, the Reformist Faction should totally let you fight for it!"

Zhang Yu thought to himself, "Aspiration for the Immortal Dao... Do I really have such a thing?"

As an otherworldly visitor from Earth, Zhang Yu wasn't sure if he actually had this in his heart, or if he had been influenced by it.

But one thing he could be sure of was that even without the influence of the Dao Lineage's Aspiration for the Immortal Dao, he had long since made up his mind on his journey from the First Layer of Kunxu.

He wanted to reach the pinnacle of the Immortal Dao, find the answer to Kunxu, and change the Kunxu world with his own will.

Immortal Slayer thought silently, "Changing the world... This is a determination that naturally condensed as Zhang Yu's cultivation, wealth, and power grew. This is... the heart of a powerhouse. It's also a thought that every Immortal, every Immortal Emperor, would similarly develop."

"It is just that during this journey of ascending, one's thoughts will gradually change. Especially after being twisted by the Aspiration for the Immortal Dao Dao Lineage, every Immortal Emperor who reaches the pinnacle often thinks about how to transform the world to uplift themselves."

"But Zhang Yu... he might be different," Immortal Slayer thought silently in his heart. "I must constantly remind him, I cannot let him make a wrong step and become another Myriad Laws Immortal Emperor."

Sect Examination Clone: "Right, what is wrong with the Myriad Laws Immortal Emperor!"

Auspicious God said: "Exactly! To become an Immortal Emperor is to violate Kunxu! Who cares about all your this and that."

Just as Zhang Yu's head started getting noisy with arguments again, Fa Liuyuan in front of him said: "I have already communicated with the two Immortals, and we will apply for a Law Establishment quota for you next year."

"But you have to promise us two things."

Hearing this, Zhang Yu immediately listened earnestly.

"First," Fa Liuyuan said, "you must complete the ten divine light transformations before you can break through to the Void Refinement Realm. You must not break through prematurely without completing the ten divine light transformations just for this opportunity for Law Establishment."

Zhang Yu knew that fully cultivating the ten divine light transformations represented a more solid foundation and a higher upper limit. Breaking through to Void Refinement after achieving this was already in his plans, so naturally, he had no objections and immediately nodded in agreement.

Fa Liuyuan continued, "Second, if you successfully break through to Void Refinement and participate in the Law Establishment competition, do not force yourself during the process. If you encounter a dangerous situation and we ask you to withdraw, you must withdraw immediately to avoid unnecessary losses, and calmly prepare for the next Law Establishment."

Although he felt Zhang Yu's determination and boldness, and affirmed Zhang Yu's ideas, in Fa Liuyuan's view, the preparation time Zhang Yu had to succeed in this fierce Law Establishment competition was ultimately too short, and the success rate was still too low.

Fa Liuyuan said, "Consider defeat before considering victory; only then can you go further on the Immortal Dao path. Do you understand, Zhang Yu?"

Seeing Zhang Yu agree, Fa Liuyuan also nodded in satisfaction, thinking to himself, "Even if the first Law Establishment is unsuccessful, using this pressure to improve his cultivation efficiency is not a bad choice."

Zhang Yu naturally would not choose this kind of behavior, which would become a stain on his future and might even cause him to be targeted by the Tax Enforcement Army. After all, he currently had over 12 million Immortal Coins in his account and was not in need of money for the time being.

The other method was to wait until the industries under Zhang Yu's own control developed vigorously, and then use the Tax Exemption Gold Token naturally.

And to use this method, naturally, the more his own industry developed and the higher the income, the greater the profit the Tax Exemption Gold Token could yield.
