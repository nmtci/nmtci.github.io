# Chapter 885 Level 3 Blazing Sun Meridian, A Pool of People Giving Chase

As Zhang Yu infused magic power into Xuan Ge from afar, the muscles throughout Xuan Ge's entire body violently vibrated. In just an instant... his entire body's meridians and Dantian swelled as if they were going to explode.

For Zhang Yu, whose magic power quality had currently reached the 500% Spirit Transformation limit and who possessed a full 1,000,000 units of magic power... just injecting a trace of his magic power was enough to completely overstuff Xuan Ge, who had just entered the Golden Core stage and whose magic power had only just broken past 1,000.

Xuan Ge's face instantly turned flushed red, and even began to turn purple, as he subconsciously said, "Minister, I... I can't hold on..."

Watching Xuan Ge struggling violently, Zhang Yu said, "I am here to help you elevate your magic power. Go all out to digest it. Do not be afraid of getting hurt, I am here."

Hearing Zhang Yu's words, the anxiety that had just instinctively swelled up in Xuan Ge's heart was swept away in a flash.

"Right! This is the sect. With Minister Zhang here, what do I have to be afraid of?"

Thinking of this, a surge of confidence spontaneously arose, and the Blazing Sun Magic Power within his body began to fiercely circulate.

"I can't let Minister Zhang look down on me!"

Xuan Ge felt a surge of determination in his heart and had already fully mobilized his Blazing Sun Meridian, completely unafraid of exposing his abnormalities.

Not to mention that the utility of his mutated high-temperature magic power was average to begin with and had long been completely exposed in the lower realm, what kind of figure was Minister Zhang right in front of him? In Xuan Ge's eyes, anything he possessed was simply not worth mentioning to the other party.

At this moment, recalling all the struggling over the past few years, the suppression he faced in the laboratory, the contempt of his teachers, and the insults of his classmates... Xuan Ge had only one thought.

"I must desperately hold on to this opportunity given by Minister Zhang!"

"This might be the most important... opportunity for me to climb upward in my entire life."

With the full operation of the Blazing Sun Meridian, Xuan Ge's body temperature continuously skyrocketed as he tried his best to digest that trace of magic power infused by Zhang Yu.

At the same time, Zhang Yu was also observing Xuan Ge's changes.

Back then, after Zhang Yu used the Dao Seed Register to copy Xuan Ge's Dao Seed, the Blazing Sun Meridian, he felt that its functions... such as enhancing the circulation efficiency of Gang Qi, improving physical movement efficiency, and its destructive heating effect, were all just average in usefulness.

After assisting Xuan Ge a few more times, Zhang Yu was too busy to pay much attention to Xuan Ge's Dao Seed. Xuan Ge, however, silently continued to raise his magic power quality to 200% and also upgraded the Blazing Sun Meridian to level 2.

But at that time, Zhang Yu just glanced at it and lost even more interest in the Blazing Sun Meridian.

The effect of the level 2 Blazing Sun Meridian was to burn one's own vital energy and flesh to release high temperatures and increase destructive power.

It looked like a combat-oriented ability, which did not fit the needs of the current Kunxu version.

Zhang Yu tried it out and found that the conversion efficiency was too low, and the cost-effectiveness was also too poor. Long-term use would even have the side effect of reducing lifespan.

At that time, Zhang Yu had an endless amount of things to do and endless directions for improvement, so he temporarily gave up exploring the Blazing Sun Meridian.

It was not until this moment, with the opening of the Law Aperture Chapter, that Zhang Yu felt the Blazing Sun Meridian on the Dao Seed Register could be recycled and utilized.

Now, Zhang Yu had 32 clones and the support of a Void Refinement-level Calculation Pool. Every minute and every second, he was completing various cultivation and learning tasks.

To him, giving some guidance to Xuan Ge in front of him would not cost much resources and time at all, yet it could yield a stronger Dao Seed and, through the Technique Aperture, make Xuan Ge feed back into a Chain Formation Diagram.

At this moment, as Xuan Ge fully circulated the Blazing Sun Meridian, his physical performance improved slightly, which in turn accelerated his speed of digesting the magic power.

Seeing Xuan Ge working hard to digest the magic power he had infused into him, Zhang Yu nodded and then said, "Next, I will instruct you on the breathing technique. Learn while you digest the magic power, do not stop."

Thus, with Zhang Yu's guidance, Xuan Ge's expression changed in shock as he felt the efficiency of his breathing technique suddenly skyrocket!

Meanwhile, Zhang Yu looked into Xuan Ge's Technique Aperture, where he had already implanted the Golden Core Sacred Body. Zhang Yu had mostly stopped using his Golden Core Sacred Body before unlocking the Infinite Chain Formation Diagram, so it was currently only at ten layers.

But even so, it could fully double one's cultivation efficiency and effects. For Xuan Ge... that was a heaven-defying result. It made him feel as if his speed of digesting magic power had surged dramatically, and the magic power that had been faintly running rampant inside his body was easily calmed a moment later.

"Is this... Minister Zhang's level?" Xuan Ge looked at Zhang Yu in shock, completely unprepared for a few words of guidance from the other party to be so profoundly enlightening, causing his cultivation efficiency to soar.

However, when Xuan Ge thought of the Spirit Transformation Realm and the Immortal Legacy, and looked at the Former Days Salary King title above the other party's head, he was immediately relieved. He felt that it was completely normal for a super heavenly chosen of a sect like him to be able to benefit him endlessly with just some casual guidance.

Zhang Yu nodded and said, "For the next period of time, come over every day to receive my magic power, and put all your effort into using your breathing technique to absorb it."

Hearing Zhang Yu's words, Xuan Ge looked at Zhang Yu in even more disbelief.

In Xuan Ge's mind, a sect heavenly chosen like Zhang Yu was absolutely a very busy person, and the value of every second of his time was beyond his imagination.

Especially after Minister Zhang announced to the public that he was going into secluded cultivation, Xuan Ge originally thought that receiving some guidance today was already a great heaven-sent opportunity, but he did not expect...

"Minister, you... you mean you will infuse magic power into me every day? I can come over every day?"

Seeing Zhang Yu nod, Xuan Ge was so excited at this moment that his face turned red, and under the operation of the Blazing Sun Meridian, bursts of high-temperature white steam continuously emanated from his entire body.

In the following days, Xuan Ge came to Zhang Yu's place every day to receive a magic power infusion, and then spent the entire 24 hours doing his breathing technique. And in order to reduce the side effects of the Blazing Sun Meridian, Zhang Yu would also occasionally mix in a trace of Profound Cold Magic Power to lower Xuan Ge's body temperature.

During this period, Xuan Ge had also pondered why Zhang Yu was helping him so much, but feeling his own magic power advancing by leaps and bounds, he quickly threw this thought to the back of his mind.

As long as he could continue to climb on the immortal path, he did not care how Zhang Yu wanted to use him.

In the blink of an eye, more than a month passed. With the infusion of Zhang Yu's magic power and the blessing of the sect's various superior conditions, Xuan Ge's magic power quality had already increased to 260%, meeting the requirements for the Blazing Sun Meridian to upgrade again.

Boom!

Accompanied by the temperature of the magic power in his body rising once more, Xuan Ge could feel his magic power ushering in another qualitative change.

Meanwhile, Zhang Yu looked at the level 3 Blazing Sun Meridian on the Dao Seed Register and began to sense and test its abilities.

As the surging high-temperature magic power circulated within Zhang Yu's body, waves of heat swept out, and he finally understood the level 3 effect of the Blazing Sun Meridian.

"Burn vital energy, at the cost of heavily injuring oneself or even burning lifespan, to increase a person's overall power output."

Zhang Yu tried it a little and discovered that under the circulation of the Blazing Sun Meridian, the scope of the power output increase was very broad.

First, his physical strength, after activating the power of the Blazing Sun Meridian, could break through the Spirit Transformation limit and temporarily reach level 51.

Zhang Yu's eyes lit up, and he thought to himself, "What if it is paired with the Strategic-Tier Technique, the Kunpeng Swallowing Heaven Manual?"

The Kunpeng Swallowing Heaven Manual was a cultivation art he obtained by passing the cultivation art test in the Martial Arts Pavilion. Besides being able to rapidly improve his basic stats, it could also use Overlimit Development to temporarily break the upper limit of those basic stats.

Thus, when Zhang Yu circulated the Kunpeng Swallowing Heaven Manual again, Overlimit Development and the Blazing Sun Meridian took effect simultaneously, unexpectedly forcing his physical strength to break through from 50.00 points all the way to 53.22 points in a single breath!

Zhang Yu knew that this was 3.22 points beyond the Spirit Transformation limit, and after breaking a cultivation realm limit, the difficulty of increasing by even 0.01 points rose exponentially.

Although Zhang Yu quickly felt that his physical body could hardly sustain it, with injuries continuously breaking out inside him, and he swiftly stopped the full-power circulation of the cultivation art and the Blazing Sun Meridian, he still felt astonished.

"Incredible, circulating the Blazing Sun Meridian and the Kunpeng Swallowing Heaven Manual together actually produces such a miraculous effect?"

Zhang Yu's heart stirred: "Then after I break through to the Void Refinement Realm, I'll be able to use this method to close the gap in basic attributes... between me and those veteran Void Refinement cultivators."

Zhang Yu knew that once he broke through to Void Refinement, without the constraints of his cultivation realm limit, the physical strength he could temporarily surge to would be even higher.

And besides the increase in physical output, Zhang Yu discovered that the Blazing Sun Meridian also had an effect on magical artifacts.

"Injecting the magic power of the Blazing Sun Meridian into a magical artifact can increase its output and temporarily enhance its performance."

"However, the side effects of the Blazing Sun Meridian will also accelerate the wear and tear of the magical artifact. If used for too long, it will cause the magical artifact to malfunction or even be destroyed..."

But Zhang Yu's eyes quickly lit up as he thought: "But I'm not afraid of destroying magical artifacts that I generate with Empress Wa Creations."

Accompanied by waves of Blazing Sun Magic Power being injected, Zhang Yu directly generated a Supreme Yang Radiance God using the Blazing Sun Magic Power.

After some testing, Zhang Yu found that under the augmentation of the Blazing Sun Meridian, the performance of the Supreme Yang Radiance God had been enhanced, impressively breaking through the original 50% limit of Empress Wa Creations to reach a full 70% of the original physical magical artifact.

"Good!" Zhang Yu's heart jolted, and his expression became increasingly excited. "To think that under the amplification of the Blazing Sun Meridian, it could actually break the performance limit of Empress Wa Creations?"

In Zhang Yu's view, this was even more useful than the enhancement to his physical strength.

After all, the temporary enhancement of physical strength was rarely used in normal times, and it also had the side effect of reducing his lifespan. Even with the Profound Cold Meridian to make up for it a little, he would ultimately still lose some lifespan.

Instead, this performance enhancement for magic power-type magical artifacts meant that Zhang Yu's products would be more competitive in the magical artifact rental market, directly increasing Zhang Yu's income starting from this month.

As for the side effects of the Blazing Sun Meridian on the magical artifacts... the magical artifacts from Empress Wa Creations were temporary items to begin with. Even if their duration was reduced to a maximum of four hours after the performance enhancement, it was no big deal to simply generate them again.

This level 3 Blazing Sun Meridian made Zhang Yu feel like a tiger that had grown wings, and even Xuan Ge in front of him became more pleasing to the eye.

Right at this moment, a message arrived from Fa Liuyuan, asking, "The time for the third-generation Law Edict has been finalized. Are you confident in breaking through to Void Refinement by May of this year?"

Looking at Fa Liuyuan's reminder, Zhang Yu turned his head to look at the Primordial Spirit in the Spirit Transformation Pool, thinking to himself, "May of this year?"

"Then I need to hurry up a bit more."

Zhang Yu knew in his heart that if he could not break through to Void Refinement, no matter how much his other abilities improved, he would still miss this law establishment.

Then did he have the confidence to complete the breakthrough?

He did!

Because at this moment, he was not charging at Void Refinement alone, but with the full assistance of his companions, pushed by Magnetic Pole, Yu Xinghan, Bai Zhenzhen, Zhang Pianpian... this pool of Spirit Transformation cultivators, making a sprint toward Void Refinement!
