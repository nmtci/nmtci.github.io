# Chapter 854: Dharma-Warding Talisman, Three Year Period, The Reincarnation Immortal Emperor's Favor Can Never Be Fully Repaid

The tenth layer of Kunxu.

Yu Xinghan descended together with Magnetic Pole, Old Gao, Shi Huaiyu, Feng Tingting, Yan Qianji, and the others. Feeling the countless streams of information flowing in the Spirit Realm, a wave of emotion surged in their hearts for a moment.

Almost two years had passed since Yu Xinghan joined the sect. He hadn't expected to return so soon, and in the capacity of an examiner and interviewer for the Sect Examination, returning to the Lower Realm.

"Let's go, we'll return to Myriad Laws University first."

The group rode a flying boat, quickly piercing through layers of clouds and sky, arriving at the Myriad Laws University campus on the Kunxu Fifth Layer.

On the vast land, rumbling construction sites could be seen everywhere, along with tall towers reaching into the clouds.

Yu Xinghan knew those were signal towers used to transmit the Dharma Realm.

Starting from the year they joined the sect, all the major universities from the second to fifth layers began reconstruction plans.

This reconstruction was partly to repair the devastation caused by the University War, and partly to respond to the technological innovations passed down from the Ten Great Sects.

For instance, Myriad Laws University prepared to expand the Dharma Realm, spreading it across the entire campus.

As far as Yu Xinghan knew, the ultimate goal of the Myriad Laws Sect was probably to connect the Dharma Realm across all layers from 1 to 10, and eventually link it with the sect's Dharma Realm.

Yu Xinghan thought to himself, "When that time comes... I'm afraid the entire 1st to 20th layers of the Dharma Realm will become one entity, completely under the control of the Myriad Laws Sect."

However, Yu Xinghan knew that the execution of this plan would inevitably face heavy resistance, and there was no telling when it would finally be completed.

Due to the reconstruction plans of various sects, currently, all the universities were undergoing massive construction, and related industries were experiencing a booming development.

Yu Xinghan's gaze swept over the information from major universities, thinking to himself, "Over the past two years, the admission scores for the Civil Engineering major at various universities have increased a lot, and they are recruiting more students than before."

This seemed very normal to Yu Xinghan. With the arrival of a new era of massive infrastructure construction, related industries needed a lot of manpower. Talent was in short supply, jobs for Civil Engineering graduates were easier to find, and even incomes were much higher than in previous years.

"Even the massage industry is booming alongside it, and the Joyful Union Sect's performance has improved significantly."

Seeing that the industries he had participated in and yearned for when he first came to the university were becoming more prosperous, Yu Xinghan couldn't help but sigh with emotion.

"Furthermore..." Yu Xinghan thought to himself, "With successful cases of entering the sect like Zhang Yu and me, I'm afraid that in the eyes of many university students, Civil Engineering graduates also have a good chance of being admitted into the sect."

Thinking of this, Yu Xinghan could not help but smile faintly: "If it was before, maybe this could be considered a misunderstanding."

"But this time, after I finish recruiting people, I'm afraid our Civil Engineering Department... will truly become the Apex of Civil Engineering in the eyes of all the major universities."

Thinking of the lower-class major that everyone originally looked down upon gradually becoming a top-level major through the struggles of himself and Zhang Yu, Yu Xinghan could not help but laugh again.

Right at this moment, Magnetic Pole said from the side, "We still have to go meet some old friends to prepare for the Sect Examination matters, so let's part ways here."

Yu Xinghan nodded, "Then I'll see you at the Examination Field later."

The group then parted ways.

---

Inside an office at Myriad Laws University.

A line of information suddenly popped up before True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter's eyes.

This director of the Security Department, who was once known as a famous peacemaker at Myriad Laws University and a Nascent Soul True Monarch who slaughtered in all directions during the University War, now looked at the familiar avatar lighting up on his contact list, a look of astonishment flashing across his face.

A moment later, True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter and the director of the Medical Department, True Monarch Miraculous Hand, crossed the Spirit Realm together and arrived at a conference room.

As soon as he saw the familiar figures in the conference room, True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter enthusiastically shouted, "Old Headmaster! Why are you back? Has the training already ended?"

Magnetic Pole waved her hand and said, "No rush, wait until the people from the other universities arrive, then we will talk about it together."

Under the surprised gazes of True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter and True Monarch Miraculous Hand, Heavenly Sword University, Vajra University, Heavenly Demon University, Netherworld University, White Bone University... except for Celestial Monsters University and Pill Cauldron University, almost every one of the remaining eight universities had two or three Nascent Souls arrive.

And when they saw Magnetic Pole and Old Gao who had returned to the Lower Realm, they also showed surprised expressions one after another, inquiring about the reason for their return to the Lower Realm.

Looking at the numerous Nascent Soul Cultivators present, Magnetic Pole spoke, "Old Gao and I returned this time to participate in the latest Sect Examination."

Just this single sentence from Magnetic Pole caused a massive uproar at the scene.

However, in front of the two Spirit Transformations, none of the numerous Nascent Soul Cultivators spoke their minds out loud; instead, various thoughts surged in their hearts.

"What? Participate in the Sect Examination?"

"With the age of Magnetic Pole and Divine Monarch Greenwood, they can still participate in the Sect Examination?"

"Could it be that the rules of the Sect Examination have changed?"

Obviously, regarding Magnetic Pole's words, the Nascent Souls of the various universities present found it somewhat hard to believe.

After all, the age division of the Sect Examination was a rule set by the sects. Although Magnetic Pole and Greenwood were considered big shots among the Ten Great Universities, inside the sect they were not even the lowest-level Sect Staff. Within the sect, they were the lowest amongst the lower layers cultivators, so how could they possibly change the rules of the Sect Examination?

Although the crowd did not speak, Magnetic Pole already sensed the doubt in their hearts. She snorted coldly, spread her five fingers, opened her palm, and said, "Just look at what this is."

Before Magnetic Pole's voice even faded, an Exam Admission Ticket slowly rose from her palm. If it was not the Exam Admission Ticket for the latest Sect Examination, what else could it be?

"An Exam Admission Ticket? It's actually an Exam Admission Ticket? How could it be an Exam Admission Ticket?"

"Is it really the Exam Admission Ticket for this session's Sect Examination?"

"You two Divine Monarchs! Are you.... really able to participate in this Sect Examination?!"

Including True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter and True Monarch Miraculous Hand, the Nascent Souls from the various universities called over by Magnetic Pole and Old Gao this time all shared one characteristic. That was, their ages placed them as older Nascent Souls, but if they achieved Spirit Transformation, they would be considered young Spirit Transformation cultivators.

Because of their status as older Nascent Souls, none of them had the qualifications to participate in the Sect Examination.

It was to the point that True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter had even considered reincarnation just to participate in the Sect Examination.

At this moment, the Exam Admission Ticket in Magnetic Pole's hand seemed to exude a breathtaking charm, attracting the gaze of every Nascent Soul True Monarch present, filling them with intense longing and making their thoughts surge violently.

Magnetic Pole went on to explain, "This was because when we were doing training up there, we were transferred to work as outsourced staff. By chance, we gained the appreciation of the higher-ups of the Myriad Laws Sect and were specially approved by the Myriad Laws Palace Hall to have the qualification for the Sect Examination."

"Right now, besides us, all the Spirit Transformation Divine Monarchs from other universities who originally participated in the training have also become Outsourced Cultivators of the Myriad Laws Sect, wanting to strive for the opportunity to get an Exam Admission Ticket."

Everyone was greatly shocked upon hearing this, feeling both envious and emotional. They could understand the choice of the Spirit Transformation cultivators from the various universities, because if it were them, they would definitely fight for the Exam Admission Ticket too. Even if the chance was slim, it was at least a chance.

Magnetic Pole continued, "We have already discussed it with Divine Monarch Sinful Lotus and the others. They will recommend you to become the new Spirit Transformation cultivators of the Ten Universities, and in the future, you will also have the opportunity to participate in the Upper Realm training."

Hearing this, True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter's spirit was roused, "Spirit Transformation?"

He sighed in his heart. After becoming a Nascent Soul cultivator, he had been stuck at this stage for so many years. During the University War, he had fought tooth and nail for a Spirit Transformation Certificate, yet he hadn't expected that now, with just a few words from Magnetic Pole, the future of so many Spirit Transformation cultivators had been decided.

"No, it's not just Magnetic Pole..." True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter analyzed in his heart. "It's Magnetic Pole and the Spirit Transformation cultivators of the other universities, but they are only the middle layer. It should be that a higher level of power in the sect influenced them, which then transmitted down to us."

At the same time, the other Nascent Soul cultivators present became slightly excited, and for a moment, all kinds of thoughts flooded their minds.

"We can become Spirit Transformation cultivators!"

"Then do we also have a chance to become outsourced worked in the sect in the future, obtain Exam Admission Tickets? Participate in the Sect Examination?"

"First become a Spirit Transformation cultivator, go up for training, then find a way to become an outsourced worker, then get the Exam Admission Ticket, and participate in the Sect Examination... Isn't this another path to join the sect?"

Just as everyone's thoughts were flying, a coercive pressure came from the Spirit Realm, and Yun Zhiguang's projection crashed down.

"Magnetic Pole, Greenwood, after you two returned to the Lower Realm, why didn't you come to report to me immediately?"

Yun Zhiguang, as a Sect Staff dispatched by the Myriad Laws Sect and the general person-in-charge of the University Dharma Realm Construction Project, had been the true controller of Myriad Laws University for the past two years, and her prestige was widely known among the major universities.

Aside from the Sect Staff dispatched by other sects to preside over work, almost no one in the entire Lower Realm dared to defy her.

Seeing Yun Zhiguang's sudden descent with such an aggressive posture, True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter and True Monarch Miraculous Hand were also startled.

Magnetic Pole and Old Gao, however, were completely unhurried. They merely displayed their Exam Admission Tickets again and explained to Yun Zhiguang, "We came down to participate in the Sect Examination, not to return to work at Myriad Laws University. Theoretically, we are not under your control, so naturally, we do not need to report to you."

Seeing the appearance of these Exam Admission Tickets, Yun Zhiguang was also stunned, her mind full of doubts.

For the past two years, because she had stayed in the second to fifth layers to preside over the work in the Lower Realm, Yun Zhiguang would not constantly cross the layers to connect with the sect.

Generally, she would contact the sect every once in a while to obtain some information from within the sect, lest she fall behind on the news.

And the last time she contacted the sect to read the news, the Heaven Mending incident at the Former Days Graveyard had not yet occurred, let alone Zhang Yu receiving a reward.

Therefore, at this very moment, Yun Zhiguang naturally did not know the origin of the Exam Admission Tickets in the hands of Magnetic Pole and Old Gao before her.

However, she had previously heard about Zhang Yu obtaining the Immortal Legacy. But in Yun Zhiguang's view, Zhang Yu was already a true Sect Staff, and had probably long since severed his past ties with Lower Realm cultivators.

"Impossible!" Yun Zhiguang thought to herself. "Suppressing the number of Lower Realm cultivators and tightening the admission quota for Lower Realm cultivators in the Sect Examination is the consensus of most cultivators in the sect... How could the higher-ups possibly give these Lower Realm Spirit Transformation cultivators exam qualifications?!"

In Yun Zhiguang's eyes, what kind of nobodies were Magnetic Pole and Old Gao? Were they worthy of making the higher-ups make an exception to issue Exam Admission Tickets?

However, as a veteran Sect Staff of many years, she still maintained her caution. She did not express the doubts in her heart, but instead said with a scrutinizing gaze, "These Exam Admission Tickets... I wonder which leader issued them to you?"

Magnetic Pole smiled faintly and said, "Lest you not believe us, Senior Yun, it is better to let another Sect Staff talk to you."

"Another Sect Staff?" Yun Zhiguang's heart stirred. "Could it be Zhang Yu?"

Just as Yun Zhiguang's mind was racing, a projection descended, and Yu Xinghan had arrived with a bang.

Yun Zhiguang was slightly stunned. Upon seeing Yu Xinghan's Rank 2 Sect Staff badge, her eyes flickered, and she thought to herself, "He's already a Rank 2 Sect Staff?"

Yun Zhiguang asked, "Yu Xinghan... why have you come to the Lower Realm?"

Yu Xinghan smiled faintly and said, "Senior Yun, I came to the Lower Realm to handle some work for the Sect Examination."

"I heard you have some doubts about the Exam Admission Tickets of Magnetic Pole and Greenwood? Let me explain it then."

"The matter is actually quite simple..."

As he spoke, Yu Xinghan released a light screen, broadcasting the scene of Fa Liuyuan conferring rewards upon Zhang Yu and the others, leaving Yun Zhiguang dumbfounded.

Particularly with the appearance of Fa Liuyuan, while the other Nascent Soul Cultivators present had little reaction, Yun Zhiguang was greatly shocked.

This was a Rank 10 Sect Staff, a cultivator at the Ascension Realm, and a popular candidate for the future Ascension Selection. To Yun Zhiguang, he was an important figure she normally had no chance to interact with.

It could be said that among everyone present, only she, Yun Zhiguang, understood the sheer weight of Fa Liuyuan's status. Even Yu Xinghan, Magnetic Pole, and Old Gao did not comprehend it as profoundly as she did.

"Saving a Minor Realm? Heaven Mending Merit?"

"Superior Merit? Myriad Markets Great Peace?"

The subsequently awarded Death-Exempting Golden Token and multiple positions simultaneously given to Zhang Yu made Yun Zhiguang's heart tremble as she watched.

As for the following scene where Old Gao was recognized by Heavenly Monarch Fa and received the exam admission ticket by having the crown of his head stroked alongside Magnetic Pole, it caused the thoughts in Yun Zhiguang's mind to churn violently.

"He was actually handed the exam admission ticket personally by Heavenly Monarch Fa?"

"This Gao Chongguang... he actually knows Heavenly Monarch Fa?"

Just as Yun Zhiguang was shocked in her heart, Magnetic Pole said lightly, "Sigh, Old Gao, it's a good thing I made you go and add Heavenly Monarch Fa as a friend after the reward ceremony, right? You were acting all bashful back then. If I hadn't forced you to add him, wouldn't you be dying of regret right now?"

Yun Zhiguang snapped her head around, extremely surprised in her heart. "He has actually added Heavenly Monarch Fa as a friend?"

At this moment, the way she looked at Magnetic Pole and Old Gao completely changed.

At the same time, as the footage quickly finished playing, Yu Xinghan spoke up, "Heavenly Monarch Fa said it well. We need to carry forward the civil engineering spirit of building Kunxu and protecting Sect Assets. I came down this time to prepare for the Sect Examination work, and I just wanted to find more cultivators who embrace the civil engineering spirit."

Yun Zhiguang hurriedly said: "Lord Fa is absolutely right, especially the profound exposition on the spirit of civil engineering. Our current reconstruction work desperately needs this spirit of civil engineering, and it will be of great help to my upcoming university management work. I only regret not being able to listen to it in person."

"I will definitely organize cultivators at all levels to properly study the spirit of civil engineering taught by Lord Fa."

Although the Nascent Soul cultivators present still did not clearly understand Fa Liuyuan's true position in the sect, they all gained some understanding upon seeing Yun Zhiguang's reaction.

"This Lord Fa is definitely an extraordinary big shot in the sect."

"To think that in such a short time, Zhang Yu has already achieved such success in the sect."

"Could it be that the Spirit Transformations of the various universities have all gone to work as outsourced staff under Zhang Yu?"

On the other side, True Monarch Heavenly Slaughter sighed in his heart: "This Lord Fa is absolutely a high-ranking official in the sect. Zhang Yu... how many years has it been since he joined the sect? He has already latched onto such a big shot, even bringing along Magnetic Pole and Old Gao to participate in the sect examination and become sect staff?"

Magnetic Pole took in everyone's reactions and thought to herself: "In this way, if the Nascent Souls of the various universities present achieve Spirit Transformation in the future, they can serve Zhang Yu as well. Many talents from the Ten Universities can support Zhang Yu's advancement."

---

Just as the new Sect Examination in the Lower Realm gradually began, Zhang Yu's influence was gradually being transmitted to the Lower Realm.

Zhang Yu was currently looking at a message sent by Su Lengyou.

"Su Lengyou: Do you have time to talk?"

"Su Lengyou: It can be in your Immortal Grotto-Heaven."

Zhang Yu's Immortal Grotto-Heaven, being a territory under his own control, did not have the Spirit Realm's surveillance and provided ample security. The other party's choice to talk here clearly meant she wanted to discuss something confidential.

Zhang Yu thought for a moment and replied, "Sure."

Su Lengyou replied, "I wonder if I can bring a friend?"

A moment later, Zhang Yu looked at Su Lengyou and Kuang Tianqing walking into the Immortal Grotto-Heaven, a trace of surprise still in his eyes.

Immortal Slayer analyzed, "I see. Kuang Tianqing is a member of the Reincarnation Group. It seems the reason Su Lengyou was able to extricate herself from the last Supreme Truth Immortal Clan incident was because she sought refuge with the Reincarnation Group."

Hearing this, Fu Ji hurriedly scrambled to continue the analysis, "When she was willing to lend us a hand during the Heaven Collapse, it seems she also received instructions from the Reincarnation Group. It looks like the Reincarnation Immortal Emperor also has ideas about the Former Days Graveyard."

Zhang Yu's mind spun like lightning as he greeted them at the same time: "Peak Master Su, the last time we saw each other, we stopped the Heaven Collapse. If it hadn't been for your help, I'm afraid I would have been intercepted by President Xiao Yunhe and the others long ago. I am deeply grateful."

Then Zhang Yu nodded to Kuang Tianqing: "Brother Kuang, it's been a long time since we last met."

Kuang Tianqing smiled faintly and did not speak.

Beside him, Su Lingyou's body overflowed with demonic qi. She said indifferently: "Being able to stop the Heaven Collapse is a good thing for all sects. As a Sect Staff of the Heavenly Demon Sect, I naturally have an inescapable duty."

"Minister Zhang, I invited you here today to tell you... Now the forces of the Heavenly Demon Sect, Netherworld Palace, and White Bone Cult in the Soul Cultivator Industry of the Former Days Graveyard have decided to join forces to deal with you."

"They plan to strangle you and the Myriad Laws departments and Sect Enterprises you represent... who want to enter the Soul Cultivator Industry of the Former Days Graveyard, in the cradle."

Zhang Yu had anticipated this. After all, Myriad Laws was inserting itself into a mature market, and it was perfectly normal to arouse the siege of the forces within the market.

He just didn't expect Su Lengyou to remind him so quickly.

Su Lengyou continued, "You should already be aware of... the two business lines of the Former Days Graveyard's Soul Cultivator Industry, right?"

Zhang Yu nodded. He knew that although the structure of the Soul Cultivator Industry was very complex, generally speaking... it mainly consisted of two business lines.

One business line belonged to the living, which was the Soul Cultivation Company transforming clients into Soul Cultivators with more customized services and lower costs compared to the Righteous Gods.

This process was often accompanied by flesh and blood trades such as the Corpse Trade and Corpse Part Manufacturing, as well as finance businesses like loans and insurance.

As far as Zhang Yu knew, many living people, due to reasons like debt, the lifespan of their physical body, or insufficient medical fees, would actively sell their physical body and then take out additional loans... to customize their First Death experience. This meant taking out a loan to buy their death, striving to become an excellent Soul Cultivator so they could earn money to pay off their debts.

The other business line belonged to the dead. The Soul Cultivation Company provided various jobs for all kinds of Soul Cultivators and collected fees from them.

Seeing Zhang Yu nod, Su Lengyou continued, "Myriad Laws lacks Soul Cultivator technology, so you need to outsource the Soul Cultivator transformation service, right? They have already secretly agreed to forbid any company in the Former Days Graveyard from providing Soul Cultivator transformation services to you. Violators will be placed on the Sect Enterprise's blacklist and lose a massive number of orders."

Su Lingyou continued, "Secondly, they have also issued a notice to the Soul Cultivators of the Former Days Graveyard, strictly forbidding them from looking for work through you. Violators will also be put on their blacklist and will never again be able to get Soul Cultivator jobs provided by them."

Zhang Yu frowned slightly. In his mind, Fu Ji was already saying worriedly, "Damn it, this is first cutting off our Soul Cultivator transformation services, slashing our business with the living. Then they prevent the Soul Cultivators from coming to us, cutting off our business with the dead."

Immortal Slayer said, "It is bound to be difficult to bite off a piece of meat in an already mature Soul Cultivator industry. Especially since Myriad Laws's Immortal Dao Technology regarding Soul Cultivators is inherently inferior to Heavenly Demon, Netherworld, and White Bone."

Su Lingyou continued, "Minister Zhang, facing their joint blockade, do you have any countermeasures?"

Although Zhang Yu had some ideas in his mind, he did not take the initiative to bring them up.

Su Lingyou, however, did not seem to care whether Zhang Yu answered or not, and simply continued, "I can represent the Heavenly Demon Sect to provide Soul Cultivator technology, and then your Myriad Laws will provide the funding to establish a joint venture subsidiary together. Only by mastering sufficiently advanced Soul Cultivator technology is it possible to gain a foothold in this industry."

"Otherwise, under joint sanctions, relying on your own research and hard work, even in twenty or thirty years, you might not be able to push Myriad Laws' market share above five percent."

Zhang Yu frowned and said, "The Heavenly Demon Sect is selling out its own technology to deal with its own Soul Cultivation Company? What benefit do you get from doing this?"

Kuang Tianqing said, "A sect is nothing more than individual halls and peaks. The food still has to be divided among different pots. Isn't the Myriad Laws Sect also restricting its own Artifact Spirit Faction? What's the problem with our sect suppressing its own Soul Cultivator Faction? Rather than letting them grow stronger, it's better to ally with your noble sect and regain control of the Soul Cultivator business."

Immortal Slayer analyzed, "It seems the Reincarnation Immortal Emperor doesn't get along with the Soul Cultivator Faction. Not only will the reincarnation business be affected by the Soul Cultivator Faction, but he himself probably can't get much from them either."

Fu Ji quickly added, "Soul Cultivators are deeply rooted in Kunxu. Although they affect the reincarnation business, the Reincarnation Immortal Emperor probably also understands that given the current situation, abolishing the Soul Cultivator business is unrealistic."

"Rather than banning the Soul Cultivation business, it would be better to start fresh. Suppress the Soul Cultivation companies they can't control, and start one with us that they can. Although the reincarnation business would still be affected, at least they could profit from the Soul Cultivation business as well. That way... reincarnation in the left hand and Soul Cultivation in the right, they make money either way."

Immortal Slayer agreed, "In addition to that, helping the Myriad Laws Sect develop Soul Cultivation technology would provoke conflict within the Myriad Laws Sect between the Soul Cultivator Faction and the Artifact Spirit Faction. Whether it's Soul Cultivation or artifact spirits, I'm afraid they are both enemies of the reincarnation business. These two factions fighting each other is a good thing for the Reincarnation Immortal Emperor."

Although Kuang Tianqing's information and the analyses of Fu Ji and Immortal Slayer all made sense, Zhang Yu still felt something was amiss.

"Is this really their sole purpose?"

Zhang Yu felt it wasn't that simple.

Seeing Zhang Yu hesitating to answer, Kuang Tianqing suddenly said, "Peak Master Su, I have a few words I'd like to say to Minister Zhang in private."

Thus, Su Lingyou temporarily left the Immortal Grotto-Heaven, and Kuang Tianqing smiled faintly, "This is the newest Dharma-Warding Talisman, it lasts for three years. The higher-ups specifically asked me to deliver it to you, Minister Zhang."

Zhang Yu's eyes narrowed upon hearing this. Looking at Kuang Tianqing in front of him, his mind raced.

Before he joined the sect, he had obtained the Dharma-Warding Talisman from Immortal Slayer, which allowed him to hide some of his secrets from the various detections of the Dharma Realm.

However, according to Immortal Slayer, this Dharma-Warding Talisman would only be effective for a maximum of three years, and with the continuous updates of the Dharma Realm, it might gradually begin to fail in just two years.

Zhang Yu originally had a plan. If the Dharma-Warding Talisman failed, his main body would hide in the Immortal Grotto-Heaven all the time, and he would use his clones for daily activities. Although it would be a bit troublesome, it would still hide a lot of information about him and avoid a lot of trouble.

Taking the latest Dharma-Warding Talisman from Kuang Tianqing at this moment, Zhang Yu felt no relief, but instead a chill running down his spine.

"As expected, the Dharma-Warding Talisman in Immortal Slayer's head... was prepared for me by the Reincarnation Group, wasn't it?"

Meeting Zhang Yu's narrowed gaze, Kuang Tianqing hurriedly said, "Minister Zhang, providing you with the Dharma-Warding Talisman is just our way of showing goodwill. We absolutely have no intention of threatening you."

"After all, if you can climb high in the Myriad Laws Sect, it will be beneficial to all of us."

"Just like our cooperation this time... If you are willing to support Peak Master Su Lengyou's plan, we are also willing to offer you some extra private thanks."

In Zhang Yu's mind, the Sect Examination Clone said, "You must be loyal, Zhang Yu! The Myriad Laws Immortal Emperor's grace can never be fully repaid! We absolutely must not do anything disloyal to Myriad Laws!"

Kuang Tianqing continued, "For example... some Evil Gods?"

Fu Ji said, "The Reincarnation Immortal Emperor's grace can never be fully repaid! Agree to it! Zhang Yu!"

Zhang Yu sighed inwardly, understanding that the things given by the Reincarnation Immortal Emperor this time... were just like the blank Law Scroll given by the Myriad Laws Immortal Emperor, dangling in front of him like a lump of immortal shit. Even knowing the heavy price of biting into it, he had no choice but to swallow it whole.
