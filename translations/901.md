*As expected...* Looking at the other party's reply and recalling how Tai Yuebai had successively approached different students, Zhang Yu thought to himself, *Is the prerequisite for this guy to obtain a Dao Seed to build a good relationship with his target? It's actually a bit like the Dao Seed Register.*

Looking at Zhang Yu, who was caught between the two, Tai Yuebai thought to himself, "Hang in there, Zhang Yu."

In Tai Yuebai's view, compared to the opportunity before him, losing some Immortal Coins was nothing at all.

Tai Yuebai thought to himself, *The first third-generation Law Establishment has gathered so many heavenly chosen, as well as an ultra-high-risk Dao Seed... If I miss this opportunity, who knows how many years I'll have to wait for the next one.*
