# Chapter 899: My Automatic Cultivation Device, A Temporary Certificate

As the first Chain Formation Diagram Zhang Yu obtained after breaking through to the Void Refinement Realm, the Great Void Dao Body provided highly obvious enhancements to the recovery of the Void-Refined Primordial Spirit, the increase in the Immortalization Degree, and the practice of Void Refinement cultivation arts.

And when Bu Yingshu was slotted into the Great Void Dao Body by Zhang Yu, the first thing she felt was... the various cultivation arts circulating within her body. The content, comprehensions, and experiences of these cultivation arts pierced through her mind like striking bolts of thunder.

At this moment, Bu Yingshu felt as if all the cultivation arts she had learned in the past were suddenly integrated and mastered in an instant, becoming familiar to an unbelievable degree.

Bu Yingshu had an even stronger feeling: if she continued to circulate and practice these cultivation arts now, her efficiency would likely increase by more than two or three times.

In this instant, Bu Yingshu felt her innate talent wildly increasing! Explosively increasing! Powerfully increasing!

*What does this mean?*

*My world-shaking talents have finally elevated once again! I've ushered in a qualitative change!*

Thus, after reminding Zhang Yu not to be too shocked, Bu Yingshu prepared to test her innate talent.

However, just as she tried to circulate a cultivation art, that feeling of explosively surging innate talent rapidly faded away.

Even the profound comprehension of various cultivation arts in Bu Yingshu's heart, that feeling of complete mastery and integration, receded in the blink of an eye.

It was as if a mind full of knowledge had been drained away in a single breath, and an incomparable sense of emptiness already shrouded Bu Yingshu's mind.

*My world-shaking talent!* Bu Yingshu was horrified inwardly. *Why did it disappear? How could it disappear?!*

When she could no longer feel even the slightest trace of her surging innate talent, Bu Yingshu looked at her hands with a terrified expression. *No!*

Zhang Yu watched the drastic changes in Bu Yingshu's expression and showed timely concern, "Dao Monarch Bu, what's wrong?"

"No... I..." Bu Yingshu said with a face full of resentment and frustration, "I don't know, I don't know what happened to me!"

Zhang Yu reminded her, "Dao Monarch Bu, with your peerless talent, could it be that you had a sudden realization while reading the basic cultivation art just now?"

"Basic cultivation art?" Hearing this, Bu Yingshu's heart stirred, and she remembered the various martial dao experiments she had done in the Martial Arts Pavilion.

*If you don't understand the cause of the experimental results, repeat the experiment and gradually adjust the variables...*

Thus, all of her recent experiences immediately replayed in her mind: "Speaking of which, it's been a very long time since I last looked at something like a basic cultivation art."

"Could it be that after my innate talent improved, looking back at the basic cultivation art gave me new insights?"

Thinking of this, Bu Yingshu hurriedly read through the basic cultivation art that Zhang Yu had just transmitted to her again.

Just as Bu Yingshu was earnestly reading the basic cultivation art, Zhang Yu secretly and fiercely thrust the Great Void Dao Body into her Technique Aperture again.

"It's coming! It's here!" Bu Yingshu's body trembled slightly, but her face was filled with pleasant surprise. "This is the feeling! It's this fulfilling feeling!"

She turned to look at Zhang Yu and said, "Little Yu! Your reminder was excellent! Reading this basic cultivation art has indeed given me a lot of insights."

As she spoke, Bu Yingshu pulled Zhang Yu into a tight embrace, happily saying, "You are truly my lucky star!"

Zhang Yu quickly said, "It is all because of your outstanding innate talent and years of accumulation, Dao Monarch Bu, that you were able to gain insights from a basic cultivation art."

Bu Yingshu recalled her many years of bitter cultivation and how she was looked down upon by her father, brother, and others... She couldn't help but nod repeatedly and say, "That's right, it is indeed the result of my long years of accumulation."

Meanwhile, before Zhang Yu even had the time to enjoy the sensation of being in Bu Yingshu's embrace, he was startled by a warning and fine that suddenly popped up before his eyes.

"100,000 Immortal Coins?" Zhang Yu was shocked. "Just a hug is going to deduct 100,000 Immortal Coins from me? What the hell kind of fine is this?"

Fu Ji said, "Just a touch is only 1,000 Immortal Coins, right? A hug bumps it up to 100,000 Immortal Coins? Then how much would the fine be for dual cultivation?"

Auspicious God said, "How terrifying! Her embrace and dual cultivation are basically lethal techniques."

Meanwhile, Bu Yingshu felt Zhang Yu's body stiffen, and she immediately understood that he had been fined. Her excited mood also calmed down slightly.

She let go of him, then transferred 100,000 Immortal Coins over and said, "I'm happy today, so consider that a free hug."

With that said, Bu Yingshu had already refocused her attention on her innate talent.

At this moment, Bu Yingshu felt as if her head was filled with countless pieces of knowledge and experience. That incomparable sense of fulfillment made her confidence skyrocket, and a thought welled up in her mind.

"With such world-shaking talents! What martial arts can I not master? What skills can I not learn?!"

"From now on, in this vast Kunxu, what experiment can stop me? What conference can stump me?!"

Just as Bu Yingshu grew more and more excited at the thought and was about to practice another advanced cultivation art, Zhang Yu once again secretly extracted the Great Void Dao Body from her Technique Aperture.

*What's going on? Why is it gone again?*

Feeling her world-shaking talents being drawn away once more, a wave of panic washed over Bu Yingshu's heart. *No! Please no! Come back to me right now!*

*My innate talent!*

"Right! Repetition! The basic cultivation art!" Feeling the rapid extraction of her innate talent, Bu Yingshu hastily refocused her attention on the basic cultivation art.

And as she began to read through it again, Zhang Yu once more placed the Great Void Dao Body back.

Feeling the changes within her body, an excited flush immediately covered Bu Yingshu's cheeks. The thrilling sensation of being permeated by the peerless talent made her body tremble uncontrollably, giving her the urge to let out a few soft moans.

But at the thought that she still harbored the Great Sage Inheritance, she immediately clenched her teeth.

"No!"

Recalling the commotion caused by the power of the Great Sage Inheritance on the Eleventh Layer last time, Bu Yingshu tightly shut her mouth.

"No!"

Recalling the commotion caused by the power of the Great Sage Inheritance on the Eleventh Layer last time, Bu Yingshu tightly shut her mouth.

"This is the 15th Layer. If my voice is heard by everyone on this layer again, it will be much more serious than last time."

Thus, Bu Yingshu desperately suppressed the urge to moan, focusing all her attention on the enhancements brought by this peerless talent.

But this peerless talent seemed to be playing hide-and-seek with her. Every time she had just enjoyed a few moments of pleasure, it would quickly withdraw and vanish without a trace, leaving her feeling empty and disappointed.

On the other side, Zhang Yu looked at Bu Yingshu's disappointed expression and casually said, "Dao Monarch Bu, to gain insights just by browsing a basic cultivation art, you truly possess peerless innate talent."

Bu Yingshu nodded absentmindedly, still studying her own body.

Zhang Yu then continued, "Gaining insights just by taking a look... If you were to comprehend and cultivate it, I'm afraid the basic cultivation art you display would be even more powerful than an ordinary person's advanced cultivation art, or even an expert cultivation art..."

Hearing these words, Bu Yingshu's eyes suddenly lit up. "Cultivate it?"

*That's right. What one learns from books is ultimately superficial. The insights gained from just browsing are limited after all.*

*Perhaps right now... I just need to cultivate a basic cultivation art. From this most foundational cultivation art, I can comprehend the Immortal Dao and completely stimulate the potential that has been hidden within my body for so long.*

The more she thought about it, the more it made sense. Bu Yingshu directly began to cultivate the basic cultivation art before her.

Meanwhile, Zhang Yu, who was observing Bu Yingshu's movements from the side, immediately sent the Great Void Dao Body over.

Feeling the innate talent added to her body, Bu Yingshu cultivated even more diligently. Especially upon sensing that this time the world-shaking talent remained constantly within her body and was no longer drawn out, she became so excited that her magic power vibrated and her vital energy surged.

In the time that followed, through repeated attempts, and with the Great Void Dao Body constantly entering and exiting, Bu Yingshu gradually figured out the key to stimulating her "innate talent".

*I need to cultivate a basic cultivation art, form insights during the cultivation process, absorb those insights, and transform them into my Immortal Dao Potential.*

*This is probably the reason why my innate talent can be stimulated.*

The corners of Bu Yingshu's mouth curled up into a faint smile, and she thought to herself, *Using a basic cultivation art to consolidate my foundation and accumulate strength for a profound breakthrough? That is indeed what my innate talent should look like.*

Then she concluded in her heart, *But not every basic cultivation art is useful. I must try them to know the result.*

Selecting another basic cultivation art to practice, this time she felt the innate talent flow through her body. Bu Yingshu's figure only trembled slightly, evidently already gradually getting used to the process of Zhang Yu stuffing the Great Void Dao Body into her.

Beside Bu Yingshu, Zhang Yu watched her get more and more excited as she practiced, knowing that his goal for today had been achieved.

He knew that starting today, Bu Yingshu would step by step be reduced to his cultivation tool, immersed in the pleasure of being penetrated by his peerless talent, endlessly practicing basic cultivation arts for him.

"If only I could turn all the trainees into my automatic cultivation tools, how wonderful would that be?"

"What a pity..."

Recalling the rows of blurry Dao Seed traces in the classroom, Zhang Yu understood that this idea of his was too difficult to achieve.

After all, he had started training Bu Yingshu back when he was in the Edict Pavilion.

However, the other trainees in this law establishment training class were strangers. He had spent several days just to barely see a few Dao Seeds clearly.

Not to mention copying their Dao Seeds and then putting them into the Great Void Dao Body. And even if they were put into the Great Void Dao Body, how to guide them to practice basic cultivation arts would be another troublesome matter.

"These guys... aren't as trusting and familiar with me as Bu Yingshu, and they certainly aren't as easy to fool as she is."

"Moreover, if I'm even slightly careless and let them or the people behind them notice something amiss, the losses would outweigh the gains."

Just as Zhang Yu was pondering in his heart, Bu Yingshu looked at him from the side and suddenly remembered something.

"Right, Zhang Yu, I haven't had the chance to tell you the rest of the law establishment goal I brought up to you last time."

Having finally stabilized her innate talent thanks to Zhang Yu's reminder, Bu Yingshu found Zhang Yu increasingly pleasing to the eye. She also couldn't wait to inform him of her law establishment goal so that he could properly cooperate with and support her.

At this moment, feeling her innate talent had skyrocketed and her confidence surging, Bu Yingshu looked at Zhang Yu and said with a serious face, "Little Yu, after so many years of being a pearl covered in dust and enduring humiliation, my hard work has finally paid off. Through my comprehension of the basic cultivation art, I have taken another solid step forward on the path of the Immortal Dao."

Bu Yingshu looked at her own hands and sighed with emotion, "Even I myself can no longer explain just how strong I am now, how terrifying my aptitude is, and how astonishing my innate talent has become."

Zhang Yu nodded repeatedly and said, "Indeed, Dao Monarch Bu, I can feel that you..."

"No." Bu Yingshu waved her hand and sighed, "You simply cannot understand what kind of state I am in right now. But whatever the case, you only need to know that in this Law Establishment, my results will probably reach an unprecedented level."

"And as it happens, this session of Law Establishment is the first session for the third-generation Law Edict. Heaven is truly helping me."

She looked at Zhang Yu and asked, "Little Yu, are you willing to support me? For the two of us, together in this minor realm, to create an unprecedented, earth-shattering Law Edict?"

Zhang Yu naturally replied, "Dao Monarch Bu, I am willing."

Bu Yingshu asked again, "But a tree that stands out in the forest will surely be destroyed by the wind. My stunning performance is also very likely to trigger the apprehension of the people above, and even their suppression."

"After all, as a member of the Immortal Clan, no matter how stunningly brilliant I am, it is highly unlikely that I will be allowed to ascend and become an Immortal." "If you want to create a world-shocking Law Edict with me, the risks you face will inevitably surge. Are you still willing?"

Zhang Yu nodded earnestly and said, "Dao Monarch Bu, if it weren't for your promotion back then, I might not even be standing here right now. Being able to create history with you is my honor."

Bu Yingshu knew that, since they were not in the conference room right now, what Zhang Yu said might not be one hundred percent true.

But she hoped Zhang Yu would support her, so naturally, she didn't mind revealing her information to him in advance.

Furthermore, Bu Yingshu felt her potential was now extraordinary, and her future improvements would undoubtedly be rapid. If Zhang Yu dared to do anything to betray her...

*When the time comes, I will suppress him with a mere flip of my hand.*

Bu Yingshu nodded and said, "Then I will first tell you about what I didn't finish explaining last time."

"Heaven and Earth are impartial, all things are unequal, the Great Dao has no preferences, and each has its own nature. Natural competition is what we call Primordial Chaos."

Recalling the words of the elders in her clan, Bu Yingshu continued, "This is the core principle of my Primordial Chaos Dao Lineage. The so-called worldly Primordial Chaos means that the entire Kunxu is forever in a state of competition. This is what brought about generations of progress in Immortal Dao Technology, and what led to today's Immortal Dao Golden Age."

"But in recent years, the major factions have become deeply entrenched. The cultivators above monopolize positions, causing the sect to increasingly lack competition, and a lack of competition means a lack of vitality for progress."

Bu Yingshu said with a proud look on her face, "Therefore, my law establishment goal this time is to change this current situation."

Zhang Yu asked, "How exactly are you going to do that?"

Bu Yingshu said word by word, "One certificate, temporary certification!"

"One certificate, temporary certification?" Zhang Yu immediately thought of the 'one certificate, eternal certification' that Di Xuan had mentioned earlier.

However, he heard Bu Yingshu say, "Nowadays, some people vainly attempt to achieve 'one certificate, eternal certification,' wanting to make cultivation realm certificates transcend life and death, and cross over reincarnation."

Zhang Yu's eyes flickered as he said, "The Innate Dao Lineage seems to have this exact idea? They want one certificate, eternal certification."

"Hmph." Hearing this, Bu Yingshu gave a cold snort and said, "I saw you talking with Di Xuan earlier. He told you about wanting one certificate, eternal certification, but he didn't tell you many of the details, did he?"

"The 'one certificate, eternal certification' they want means that if they pass the exam for a Nascent Soul Certificate in this life, they will forever be at the Nascent Soul realm. If they obtain an Ascension Certificate in this life, they want to hold that Ascension Certificate for all their future lives. Even after reincarnation, they would still possess the certificate. One certificate passed down for eternity, the person dies but the certificate remains — how can that be acceptable?"

Bu Yingshu said, "I came here this time precisely to shorten the validity period of some certificates to 20 years, or even 10 years! Once they expire, they must be reassessed and compete all over again!"
